About CKA Logistics

Your trusted logistics partner in Ghana

Our Company

CKA Logistics Services is a proudly Ghanaian-owned company established in 2017, committed to delivering innovative and cost-effective logistics solutions. We specialize in the transportation of both laden and empty containers, serving a wide range of clients across industries. What sets us apart is our people—dedicated professionals from diverse business and cultural backgrounds who work closely with our clients to ensure seamless logistics operations. With years of experience, technical expertise, and a hands-on approach, we’ve grown into a trusted partner for business owners and stakeholders involved in logistics activities. At CKA Logistics Services, we’re driven by excellence and reliability. Our goal is to consistently deliver high-quality service that adds value to every partnership. When you work with us, you're not just moving goods—you're moving your business forward.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative logistics solutions that exceed customer expectations through reliable services, modern equipment, and a commitment to operational excellence.

Our Vision

To be the preferred logistics service provider in West Africa, recognized for our efficiency, integrity, and contribution to the region's economic growth.
